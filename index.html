What Does It Mean to Productize AI?

  • A model ≠ a product
  • A product = AI + UX + Infrastructure + Value
  • It transforms research into real-world use

Three Core Principles

  1. Product–Problem Fit: Solves a real need and fits into the user journey
  2. UX and Explainability: Clear and trustworthy experiences
  3. Infrastructure and Feedback: Scalable and self-improving systems

Examples of AI Products

  • Grammarly: Contextual writing assistance
  • ChatGPT: Conversational and natural AI interface
  • Spotify: Personalized music experience

Challenges in Productizing AI

  • Lack of explainability → user distrust
  • Bias in data → unfair outcomes
  • Complex integration → higher dev cost
  • Low adoption → weak UX
