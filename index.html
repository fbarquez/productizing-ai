What is Productizing AI?

  • A model ≠ a product
  • A product = AI + UX + Infrastructure + Value
  • Transforming research into real-world solutions

Three Core Principles

1. Product–Problem Fit

  • Solves a real need
  • Fits naturally into the user journey

2. UX and Explainability

  • Transparent, understandable AI
  • Builds trust and usability

3. Infrastructure and Feedback

  • Scalable, reliable system
  • Learns and improves with user data

Examples of Productized AI

  • Grammarly: Writing assistance with clear feedback
  • ChatGPT: Conversational AI with natural interaction
  • Spotify: Seamless music personalization

Challenges in Productizing AI

  • Lack of explainability → user distrust
  • Bias in data → unfair outcomes
  • Complex integration → higher dev cost
  • Low adoption → weak UX
